Programs

TeKnoWave's IT Technology programs focus on fully preparing the graduate for a wide variety of technology jobs. To accomplish this, there is a need to maintain a top standard so our aim is high. We want graduates to achieve industry certifications such as A+, Microsoft 2003 MCSA, CCNA, Unix, Oracle OCP and others as well as IT diplomas, university level certificates in management studies and degree credits that require hard work and dedication on the part of the student and of course superior instruction, support and mentoring which will be supplied by TeKnoWave partners.

Support services will be available online as well as in person. As part of the TeKnoWave program, students participate in real projects working in Aboriginal communities and firms to assure on-the-job experience is gained to practice the knowledge and skills learned in the class room.

The following programs and many more will be offered in various centres across the country according to the job demands in the local region.

NOTE: In order to continuously improve our programs, TeKnoWave Inc. and its partner's reserves the right to modify programs at any time.
